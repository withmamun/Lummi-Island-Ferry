Ferry Directions

There is no fuel station on Lummi Island. If you are driving here make sure you have enough in the tank to get around. There are no public restrooms at the mainland ferry dock or on the ferry.

Ferry Highlights

  • Extra Runs will be made if necessary to clear the Dock.
  • Subject to change without notice.
  • Adverce Weather may delay runs.
  • Crossing Time Approximately five minutes..

Ferry Information

  • Round-trip fares are collected during the passage to the island.
  • All major credit cards accepted except American Express. Debit cards are accepted as long as they have a major credit card logo.
  • Pedestrians, bicycles, and motorcycles wait near the crossing gate and load as directed.
